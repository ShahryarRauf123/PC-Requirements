These are the requirements of PC, which will smoothly run the softwares, such as Lumion, Unreal Engine 5, Twinmotion

Processor

For processor, the requirements are following

  • 3.2Ghz (Base Clock)
  • 4.5Ghz (Boost Clock)
  • 8 core processor

Motherboard

For motherboard, the requirements are following

  • 4x DDR4 RAM Slots
  • Supports Cooling
  • Support Processor, and GPU

Graphics Card

For GPU, the requirements are following

  • Must be of Nvidia
  • 8GB GDDR6
  • Ray Tracing

RAM

For RAM, the requirements are following

  • DDR4
  • 3200 Mhz/ 3.2Ghz

Power Supply

For PSU, the requirements are following

  • 80 PLUS Silver
  • Supporting Peripherals
  • Must have good cooling

Case

For Case, the requirements are following

  • Must support Motherboard
  • Must have good cooling

SSD (For OS)

For OS.SSD, the requirements are following

  • >200GB
  • Must have average to high speed

SSD (For Software)

For SSD (For Software), the requirements are following

  • > 500GB
  • Must have good speed

HDD

For HDD, the requirements are following

  • 1 TB HDD
